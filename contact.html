Accessibility Information

At FitZone, we are committed to ensuring that our facilities and services are accessible to all individuals. Our building is fully wheelchair accessible, and we have a range of staff to assist you. If you have any specific accessibility needs or require assistance, please do not hesitate to contact us using the form above or visit us in person. Our team is here to support you and ensure a comfortable experience at our gym.

Parking Information

We offer ample parking facilities for our members and visitors. There is a dedicated parking lot adjacent to the gym with spaces available on a first-come, first-served basis. Additionally, there are several public parking options nearby, including street parking and multi-story car parks. Please allow extra time during peak hours to find a parking space.
