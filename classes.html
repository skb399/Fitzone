Our Classes

Inclusive group sessions designed to support different goals, abilities, and experience levels.

HIIT

High-energy interval training combining cardio and strength. Short bursts of effort with recovery periods, with options for different fitness levels.

Intensity: Medium–High

Strength & Conditioning

Technique-focused sessions designed to build confidence with weights and resistance training, using functional and progressive movements.

Intensity: Medium

Yoga & Mobility

Low-impact sessions focusing on flexibility, balance, breathing, and recovery. Suitable for all abilities, including beginners.

Intensity: Low

Bootcamp

Social, full-body workouts combining strength and cardio. Expect motivating group sessions that encourage teamwork and community.

Intensity: Medium–High
